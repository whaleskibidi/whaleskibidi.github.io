FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Contact us for more information or to subscribe for tour updates.

What should I bring?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, a camera, warm clothing, and comfortable shoes with good grip.

What's the best time to see whales?

Whale watching season in Massachusetts runs from April through October, with peak activity in summer months.

How long are the tours?

Our tours typically last 3-4 hours, giving you plenty of time to spot and observe these magnificent creatures.
